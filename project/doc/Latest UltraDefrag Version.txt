The latest version of UltraDefrag (8.0.0) can be found at the official
UltraDefrag website:

  https://ultradefrag.net/

UltraDefrag 8.0.0 provides a universal installer which guarantees
easy installation on both 32 bit and 64 bit systems.

To get older versions of UltraDefrag (7.1.1-) please follow the link:

  https://sourceforge.net/projects/ultradefrag/files/

Follow detailed instructions provided there to choose the installer
the most appropriate for your system.
